Perspire Cologne

For this project, I created a parody cologne called Perspire, which satirizes the idea of selling a product with no real value by packaging it as something luxurious and desirable. The concept was to take an everyday, even unpleasant, thing; Sweat, and transform it into a “fancy” commodity through exaggerated branding, sleek packaging, and aspirational marketing language

For the brand identity, I focused on creating a refined yet memorable mark. The lowercase ‘r’ subtly incorporates a small sweat droplet, adding a clever visual tie to the concept. The overall typeface is sleek and elegant, reinforcing a sense of high–end luxury while keeping the design minimal and modern.

A Close up of the letter 'r' featuring a sweat droplet

The final mock-up showcases both the bottle and its accompanying case, highlighting how the identity system works cohesively across the full product experience.
